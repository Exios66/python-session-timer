Fake news is deliberately fabricated information that is presented as factual news with the intention to deceive or mislead readers.
Misinformation spread through social media that contains false or inaccurate information.
Propaganda designed to influence public opinion through biased or misleading content.
Clickbait articles with sensational headlines that exaggerate or misrepresent the actual content.
Satirical content that parodies real news but may be mistaken as factual by some readers.
Deliberately deceptive content created for political or financial gain.
False information shared without fact-checking or verification of sources.
Manipulated content that distorts facts to push a specific agenda.
Untrue stories packaged to look like legitimate news articles.
Incorrect information spread virally through social networks.
